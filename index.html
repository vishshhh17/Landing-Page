Why to invest

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency, without a central bank or single administrator, that can be sent from user to user on the peer-to-peer bitcoin network without the need for intermediaries. Bitcoins are created as a reward for a process known as mining

The first way you can invest in Bitcoin is by purchasing a coin or a fraction of a coin via trading apps such as Coinbase. In most cases, you'll need to provide personal information to set up an account, then deposit money you'll use to purchase bitcoins.
